Donut maker is a fun clicker game that can get very addicting. The goal of the game is to get as many donuts possible. Don't worry, you can give your clicking finger a break by purchasing auto clicks. This will handle the clicking for you. You can purchase as many as you want, and if you're feelin' frisky, continue clicking your mouse for even more donuts! Additionally, you should purchase donut multipliers to make your donuts worth more than 1. You can never have too many donuts, right?
